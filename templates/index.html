tuesdays for trash

Every Tuesday our movement encourages individuals all over the world to join together and pick up trash in their local area (safely and responsibly) then share it by tagging us and using the hashtags #TuesdaysForTrash, to spread the message and encourage others. These actions directly address SDGs 3, 6, 11, 12, 13 and 16.

About our vision

To mobilize communities around the world to make intersectional behavioral changes and fight for the creation of a healthier home for everyone on this planet--beginning at the local level.
